this is a remake of urban champion made with python and pygame.

plot:
-----
at the exit of a snacks bar you get attacked by a mysterious guy.
what do he want from you ?
maybe he want your snacks or he is mad at others things.
all you have to do is to defend yourself, and while doing so beware 
of the police and others hazardous things.
so, will you triumphs in your fight agains your assailant and be
the urban champion ?

how to play:
use the arrows keys to move and the following keys:
 H : normal attack
 J : strong attack
